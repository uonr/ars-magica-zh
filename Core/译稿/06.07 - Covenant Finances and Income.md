## Covenant Finances

Covenants almost always need some source of mundane wealth to obtain things like food and clothing. Even those that are entirely self-sufficient have a source of mundane wealth — it just provides food and clothing directly. It is entirely possible to play the game while leaving these matters entirely in the background, but this section is for troupes that want a bit more detail in the finances of the covenant, so that they can tell stories about increasing its wealth, or about dealing with threats to its mundane income. The *Covenants* supplement has a lot more detail on this topic, and *City and Guild* provides more general rules and background for craft and trading that covenants could also use to make money.

*结社*几乎总是需要某种凡俗财富来源，以获取食物和衣物之类的物品。即使是那些完全自给自足的*结社*，也有凡俗财富来源，只不过它直接提供食物和衣物而已。完全可以在游戏中将这些事务留在背景中处理，但本节是为那些希望对*结社*财务有更多了解的*剧团*准备的，让他们能够讲述增加财富或应对凡俗收入威胁的故事。*Covenants* 在这一主题上有更多详细内容，*City and Guild* 则提供了更通用的手工艺与贸易规则及背景，*结社*同样可以利用这些规则来赚钱。

> ### The Mythic Pound
> 
> Prices in this chapter are quoted in Mythic Pounds. This conveniently ignores the fact that Mythic Europe consists of a multitude of different realms with different coinages, whose relative value may be subject to significant variation. City and Guild and the Tribunal books contain more information on local currencies, but most sagas do not need that much detail.
> 
> One Mythic Pound is equivalent to a pound of silver, the annual income of a typical peasant, or enough money to maintain an average Hermetic laboratory for a year.

> ### 神话镑 (The Mythic Pound)
> 
> 本章的价格均以神话镑报价。这种做法方便地忽略了这样一个事实：神话欧洲由众多使用不同货币的地区组成，其相对价值可能存在较大差异。*城市与行会*以及各仲裁典籍提供了更多关于当地货币的信息，但大多数*传奇*不需要如此详细。
> 
> 一神话镑相当于一磅白银、一个典型农民的年收入，或者足以维持一座普通赫尔墨斯*工坊*运作一年的钱。

### Income

Most covenants have a single source of income, although some have diversified with two or more distinct sources, and impoverished covenants have none. Broadly speaking, a source of income is a single large enterprise which provides a significant portion (usually most or all) of the covenant's income. For the sake of simplicity, a group of related enterprises (for example, agricultural land with a variety of crops, or a trading business with multiple trade routes plus incomes from rent) can be counted as a single source. It is probably not worth accounting for any trivial sources of income (for example, selling surplus honey for a few shillings at the local market), unless they provide several pounds or more per year. Each source of income comes in one of four categories, specifying broadly how much income it provides (see insert).

### 收入 (Income)

大多数*结社*只有单一收入来源，不过有些*结社*已分散为两种或更多不同来源，而贫困的*结社*则没有任何来源。广义上说，收入来源是一项单一的大型事业，它提供*结社*收入的重要部分（通常是大部分或全部）。为了简便起见，一组相关事业（例如种植多种作物的农业用地，或拥有多条贸易路线加上租金收入的贸易生意）可以算作单一来源。任何微不足道的收入来源（例如在当地市场卖几先令的多余蜂蜜）可能都不值得计入，除非它们每年能提供数镑或更多收入。每个收入来源属于四个类别之一，大致说明它所提供的收入金额（见插栏）。

### Baseline

The base for covenant creation (a standard covenant with no Hooks or Boons relating to income) is a single Typical source of income. An alternative base option is to take two Lesser sources of income instead of one Typical source. (Although this provides a lower total income, there are benefits to such diversity. Should one source of income fail, the covenant has a backup, plus there are double the opportunities for income growth and for stories.)

This provides a fairly modest income by Hermetic standards (although a considerable sum by mundane standards), usually enough to cover the covenant's expenses. Unless a relevant Hook is taken, the source of income may be assumed to be mostly stable and discreet — something that the magi will not need to frequently concern themselves with. The annual income of 100 pounds is just a guideline, and assumes an averagesized covenant of about six magi. If your covenant has more magi, you may wish to adjust the income upwards by 15 pounds per additional magus. Likewise, if you have fewer magi, you may reduce the income by the same amount. For a covenant with only one Lesser source of income. the adjustment should be only six pounds per magus. If need be, you may also tweak the starting income relative to the starting yearly expenditure, so that at the start of the saga, the desired surplus or deficit is attained. Probably you will want to start the saga with the covenant having a small annual surplus, say 10 pounds or so, enough to cover a modicum of sundry expenses.

### 基准线 (Baseline)

*结社*创建的基准（一个没有与收入相关的羁绊或契机的标准*结社*）是单一的典型收入来源。另一种基准选择是以两个次等收入来源替代一个典型来源。（虽然这提供的总收入较低，但这种多元化有其好处。一旦某个收入来源失效，*结社*还有备用来源；此外，收入增长和故事产生的机会也翻了一倍。）

按赫尔墨斯标准衡量，这提供了一份相当适度的收入（尽管按凡俗标准已是可观的金额），通常足以覆盖*结社*的开支。除非选取了相关羁绊，否则可以假设收入来源大致稳定且不引人注目，也就是*法师*们不必经常操心的事情。100 镑的年收入只是一个指导数字，并假设*结社*规模适中，约有六名*法师*。如果你的*结社*拥有更多*法师*，可以考虑每增加一名*法师*将收入上调 15 镑。同理，*法师*人数较少时，也可以按同等幅度减少收入。对于只有一个次等收入来源的*结社*，调整幅度应仅为每名*法师* 6 镑。如有必要，也可以相对于起始年度支出微调起始收入，使*传奇*开始时达到预期的盈余或赤字。你可能希望在*传奇*开始时，*结社*拥有一个小额年度盈余，比如 10 镑左右，足以应付一些零散杂项开支。

### Sources of Income

The following sources of income can serve as inspiration for your covenant. *Covenants* contains more detail on all of these options, and any source can supply any level of income.

**Agriculture:** The foundation of medieval society, and unlikely to cause conflict unless the covenant has no right to the land it is using.

**Charity:** Someone is giving the covenant money, which is great, as long as they do not stop.

**Crime:** Hermetic magic can support some very effective robbers, and maybe the covenant can play (or even be — this is about the right period) Robin Hood.

**Fishing:** The watery equivalent of agriculture, and also unlikely to cause conflict.

**Forestry:** In addition to selling the wood, the magi might hunt and harvest the animals of the forest.

**Hospitality:** The covenant provides food, drink, and shelter to wealthy travelers.

**Livestock:** Cattle, sheep, and horses can all be raised to provide income.

**Manufacturing:** If the covenant employs enough craftsmen, it can make goods for sale.

**Mining:** Metals, salt, and coal are good, if unglamorous, sources of income. Dressed stone can also be sold. Gold or silver mines are spectacular, but may draw unwelcome attention.

**Money:** If the covenant has money already, it can invest or lend it to make more. The Church often disapproves of this, unfortunately.

**Service:** Skilled covenfolk might be able to sell their skills to others, everything from entertainers to mercenaries.

**Toll-Gathering:** A covenant that controls an important route can charge people to use it.

**Trade:** This involves moving goods from somewhere they are cheap to somewhere they are expensive, and selling them there.

**Wizardry:** Wizards can sell the products of their magic. This is looked down upon by many magi, and the Quaesitores may take an interest it, but handled well it can make a lot of money for relatively little effort.

### 收入来源 (Sources of Income)

以下收入来源可以作为你的*结社*的灵感。*Covenants* 对所有这些选项有更详细的说明，任何来源都可以提供任意等级的收入。

**农业：** 中世纪社会的基础，不太可能引发冲突，除非*结社*对其使用的土地没有合法权利。

**慈善：** 有人在给*结社*钱，这很好——只要他们不停止。

**犯罪：** 赫尔墨斯魔法可以支持一些非常高效的劫匪，也许*结社*可以扮演（甚至成为——这个时代背景正合适）罗宾汉。

**渔业：** 农业的水上等价物，同样不太可能引发冲突。

**林业：** 除了出售木材，*法师*们还可以在森林中狩猎和采集动物。

**款待：** *结社*为富裕旅行者提供食物、饮料和住宿。

**畜牧：** 牛、羊和马都可以饲养以提供收入。

**制造业：** 如果*结社*雇用了足够多的工匠，就可以生产商品出售。

**采矿：** 金属、盐和煤炭虽不华丽，却是良好的收入来源。加工过的石料也可以出售。金矿或银矿利润丰厚，但可能引来不必要的关注。

**金融：** 如果*结社*已有资金，可以通过投资或放贷来获取更多收益。遗憾的是，教会通常不赞同这种做法。

**服务：** 技艺精湛的*结社民*也许能够向他人出售技能，从艺人到雇佣兵皆有可能。

**收费通行：** 控制重要路线的*结社*可以向过路者收费。

**贸易：** 将货物从廉价之地运往昂贵之地出售。

**魔法营生：** *法师*可以出售其魔法的产物。许多*法师*对此嗤之以鼻，*监察*也可能对此产生兴趣；但处理得当，这可以以相对较少的努力赚取大量金钱。
