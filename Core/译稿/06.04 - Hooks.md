### 羁绊 (Hooks)

Hooks are features of the covenant that lead to stories. Hooks come in two kinds, Major and Minor, just as character Flaws do. However, there are no Hooks that simply weaken the covenant; they are all concerned with story ideas. A covenant may have as many Hooks as the players want, but as these choices are indications of the stories that the players want, the troupe should not take so many that some never come up. That would just disappoint the players who wanted that sort of story. Hooks do not provide Build Points. Instead, they allow the players to take Boons (see page 183) for the covenant. A Major Hook provides three points that can be spent on Boons, while a Minor Hook provides one point.

The Hooks listed below are merely examples. A Hook can be anything that the troupe thinks will make the saga more interesting, and *Covenants* provides many more examples.

羁绊是*结社*中引发故事的特征。羁绊与角色的劣势一样，分为主要和次要两种。不过，羁绊并不会单纯削弱*结社*，它们都与故事创意有关。*结社*可以拥有任意数量的羁绊，但由于这些选择代表着玩家希望体验的故事类型，*剧团*不应选取过多，以免部分羁绊永远没有机会登场，令期待这类故事的玩家失望。羁绊不提供建造点，而是允许玩家为*结社*选取契机（见第 183 页）。主要羁绊提供 3 点可用于购买契机的点数，次要羁绊提供 1 点。

以下列出的羁绊仅为示例。任何*剧团*认为能让*传奇*更有趣的要素都可以作为羁绊，*Covenants* 一书提供了更多示例。

#### 次要羁绊 (Minor Hooks)

Contested Resource: Access to one of the covenant's accessible resources is contested with someone, or something, else. Thus, getting the resource requires a story. As a rule, one story means that the resource is available for five years. The resource in question should be of sufficient importance that the magi will want to bother with the story.

This Hook may be taken multiple times to cover multiple resources. It may be Unknown when the saga starts, and only become known when the first contest comes due.

**争议资源**：*结社*某项可取用资源的使用权与他人或他物存在争议。因此，获取该资源需要经历一段故事。通常，一段故事意味着该资源可使用五年。所争夺的资源应足够重要，使*法师*愿意为之应对故事。

此羁绊可多次选取，以涵盖多项资源。它在*传奇*开始时可以是未知的，仅在第一次争夺发生时才被揭晓。

Favors: The covenant owes favors to someone or something, possibly another covenant, or possibly a mundane lord, bishop, or supernatural creature. This person cannot give the covenant orders, but the covenant is really obliged to help if they are in difficulties.

This Hook could be Unknown, if the obligations were incurred by other members of the covenant who have neglected to tell the player characters.

**人情债**：*结社*欠着某人或某物的人情，可能是另一个*结社*，也可能是凡俗领主、主教或超自然生物。该方无法向*结社*发号施令，但若对方陷入困境，*结社*实际上有义务伸出援手。

若相关义务是*结社*其他成员所欠下且未告知玩家角色的，此羁绊可以是未知的。

**Hermetic Politics:** The covenant is engaged in the elaborate game of trading of favors that passes for politics in some Hermetic tribunals. When the saga begins, the covenant owes favors to three other covenants, but is owed three favors as well. The Coenobium Rhodanien, in the Provençal Tribunal, is notorious for its involvement in Hermetic politics, preventing the foundation of any covenants that it does not control over a substantial area of the tribunal (*Faith & Flame*, page 128).

**赫尔墨斯政治**：*结社*卷入了复杂的人情交换游戏，这便是某些赫尔墨斯*仲裁*辖区中政治的运作方式。*传奇*开始时，*结社*欠其他三个*结社*的人情，但也各有三个*结社*欠它的人情。普罗旺斯*仲裁*辖区的科诺比乌姆·罗达尼恩（Coenobium Rhodanien）以其深度介入赫尔墨斯政治而臭名昭著，它阻止了任何不受其控制的*结社*在该*仲裁*相当大范围内建立（*Faith & Flame*，第 128 页）。

**Monster:** A powerful supernatural creature lives near the covenant. The creature can be aligned with any realm, and should be too powerful for the player characters to defeat at the beginning of the campaign. This Hook may be Unknown. Circulus Ruber, the oldest covenant in the Hibernian Tribunal, is plagued by a Cailleach, an evil faerie hag who would love to tear the tower down, but cannot breach its Aegis of the Hearth. She takes her frustration out on travelers to and from the covenant (*The Contested Isle*, page 81).

**怪物**：一只强大的超自然生物栖居在*结社*附近。该生物可属于任何界域，且应在战役初期强大到玩家角色无力击败的程度。此羁绊可以是未知的。希伯尼亚*仲裁*辖区历史最悠久的*结社*科尔克卢斯·鲁伯（Circulus Ruber）深受一名凯利卡（Cailleach）的困扰——这是一名邪恶的*妖精*老妪，极欲摧毁塔楼，却无法突破*炉心护盾*。她将满腔怒火发泄在来往*结社*的旅人身上（*The Contested Isle*，第 81 页）。

**Poverty:** The covenant has only one Lesser source of income, with little or no surplus. This may suffice for day to day matters, but resources for major expenses require a story. This Hook can be Unknown, but probably not for very long

**贫困**：*结社*只有一个次要收入来源，几乎没有或完全没有盈余。这或许足以应付日常事务，但重大开销所需的资源则需要故事来解决。此羁绊可以是未知的，但大概不会未知太久。

**Protector:** The covenant is responsible for protecting something, such as a village, a magical grove, or another, weaker, covenant. This Hook may be taken more than once, to represent multiple protectorates.

**保护者**：*结社*负有保护某物的责任，例如一座村庄、一片魔法树丛，或另一个更弱小的*结社*。此羁绊可多次选取，代表多个被保护对象。

**Regio:** There is a regio on the covenant site, although the covenant is not in it. The magi do not know everything that is in the regio.

If the regio is not a Magical regio, being Divine, Faerie, or Infernal, that counts as an additional minor Hook. This Hook can be taken if the covenant is in a regio; in that case, it simply refers to a second regio.

***灵阶***：*结社*所在地点存在一个*灵阶*，但*结社*并不在其中。*法师*对*灵阶*内的一切并非了如指掌。

若该*灵阶*并非魔法*灵阶*，而属于神圣、*妖精*或地狱*灵阶*，则额外算作一个次要羁绊。若*结社*本身位于某个*灵阶*内，仍可选取此羁绊，此时它仅指另一个独立的*灵阶*。

**Rival:** Someone or some group is working to undermine or stymie the covenant. This rival may be much weaker than the covenant, as long as he is capable of causing problems worthy of stories. This Hook may be Unknown.

**竞争对手**：某个人或某个团体正致力于削弱或阻挠*结社*。该对手可以远比*结社*弱小，只要他有能力制造足以构成故事的麻烦即可。此羁绊可以是未知的。

**Road:** The covenant is on an important mundane road, river, or sea route, so that people often turn up at the covenant, bringing or causing stories.

**要道**：*结社*位于一条重要的凡俗道路、河流或海路沿线，因此经常有人来访，带来或引发故事。

**Superiors:** The player characters are not in charge of the covenant, and while their superiors cannot order them about the player characters do not have control of covenant resources.

**上级**：玩家角色并不掌管*结社*，尽管他们的上级无法对他们发号施令，玩家角色也无法控制*结社*的资源。

**Urban:** The covenant is in a small market town. Many of the inhabitants are not part of the covenant, and the covenant does not rule the town. Schola Pythagoranis, in the Stonehenge Tribunal, is located in the town of Cambridge, and deeply involved with the young university there (see *Art & Academe*, page 111).

**城镇**：*结社*位于一座小型市镇之中。大多数居民并非*结社*成员，而*结社*也不统治该镇。石环*仲裁*辖区的毕达哥拉斯学院（Schola Pythagoranis）位于剑桥镇，并与那里新兴的大学有着深厚联系（见 *Art & Academe*，第 111 页）。

#### 主要羁绊 (Major Hooks)

**Beholden:** The covenant owes favors to someone or something, possibly another covenant, or possibly a mundane lord, bishop, or mystical creature. The external party can give the covenant orders, although the covenant gets to decide on the best way to carry them out.

**受制于人**：*结社*欠着某人或某物的人情，可能是另一个*结社*，也可能是凡俗领主、主教或神秘生物。外部方可以向*结社*发号施令，尽管*结社*可以自行决定最佳执行方式。

**Castle:** Castles serve three functions. A castle acts as a refuge from military forces, so it defends territory. A castle acts as a staging ground for armies, so it threatens to assault its neighbors. A castle costs a fortune to create and maintain, and in many places requires the permission of the king to build, so it communicates the status of its owner. A castle, then, is a claim to political power, backed with the threat of force.

A brigade of knights, supported by a castle, can travel about twenty miles in a day. This means they have the ability to raid targets up to ten miles away, and return to barracks before nightfall. All people who live within that radius, or have interests there, will rapidly become aware of the existence of the castle.

The temporal and spiritual authorities of an area cannot ignore a covenant that builds a castle. Covenants that hold castles must either be so secluded that there are no neighboring nobles to alarm, or must come to terms with the secular and religious powers of an area. *Covenants* provides more detail on the forms that castles can take, but this Hook applies to any structure that the local nobility see as a threat.

**城堡**：城堡具有三种功能：作为抵御军事力量的庇护所，从而守卫领地；作为军队的集结地，从而威慑邻近势力；城堡建造和维护耗资巨大，在许多地方还需要国王的许可，因此彰显其主人的地位。总而言之，城堡是以武力威慑为后盾的政治权力宣示。

以城堡为依托的骑士队伍一天可行进约二十英里，这意味着他们能够袭击十英里以内的目标，并在日落前返回营地。居住在该半径范围内或在此有利益的所有人，都会迅速意识到城堡的存在。

一个地区的世俗与宗教权威无法对建造了城堡的*结社*视而不见。持有城堡的*结社*要么必须足够偏远，以至于没有邻近贵族会受到惊扰，要么必须与当地的世俗和宗教力量达成协议。*Covenants* 一书对城堡的各种形式有更详细的描述，但此羁绊适用于当地贵族视为威胁的任何建筑结构。

**Monster:** A powerful mystical creature lives inside the covenant. The creature can be aligned with any realm, and should be too powerful for the player characters to defeat at the beginning of the saga.

**怪物**：一只强大的神秘生物栖居在*结社*内部。该生物可属于任何界域，且应在*传奇*初期强大到玩家角色无力击败的程度。

**Mundane Politics:** The covenant is deeply ensnared in mundane politics, and must keep the Quaesitores from becoming too interested in their activities.

**凡俗政治**：*结社*深陷凡俗政治之中，必须设法避免*监察*对其活动产生过多兴趣。

**Poverty:** The covenant has no source of income at all, and no mundane resources to speak of. Even providing daily food requires stories. Note that this will set the tone of at least the beginning of the saga.

**贫困**：*结社*完全没有收入来源，也几乎没有任何凡俗资源。甚至提供日常食物都需要故事来解决。请注意，这将决定*传奇*至少初期的基调。

**Regio:** There is a regio on the covenant site, although the covenant is not in it. The magi do not know everything that is in the regio, and inhabitants of some sort occasionally come out and cause problems. If the regio is not magical, that counts as an additional Minor Hook.

***灵阶***：*结社*所在地点存在一个*灵阶*，但*结社*并不在其中。*法师*对*灵阶*内的一切并非了如指掌，而且某些居民偶尔会出来制造麻烦。若该*灵阶*并非魔法*灵阶*，则额外算作一个次要羁绊。

**Rival:** Someone or some group is working to destroy the covenant, and has the resources for this to be possible. Thus, the rival must be of comparable power to the covenant. The Scholomance, in the Transylvanian Tribunal, is a school for future apprentices, agents, and companions. However, it is in a regio that is not always accessible, and has gained a reputation as a school for sorcerers, run by the devil himself. A papal legate has been ordered to investigate these rumors, and has established himself in the area (*Against the Dark*, page 91).

**竞争对手**：某个人或某个团体正致力于摧毁*结社*，并拥有实现这一目标的资源。因此，该对手必须与*结社*实力相当。特兰西瓦尼亚*仲裁*辖区的所罗门秘学院（Scholomance）是一所培训未来*学徒*、代理人和*同伴*的学校。然而，它位于一个并非总是可进入的*灵阶*之中，并已赢得了一所由魔鬼本人执掌的术士学校的名声。一名教皇使节奉命调查这些传言，并已在该地区安顿下来（*Against the Dark*，第 91 页）。

**Road:** The covenant is on an important mystical trail of some kind, so that supernatural creatures, from one or more realms, often turn up at the covenant. The covenant of Didyma, in the Theban Tribunal, is linked to the city of Miletos by the Sacred Way. This is an important mundane road, but also a major route from the faerie realm to the mundane world, and faeries often appear on it (*Through the Aegis*, Chapter 4).

**要道**：*结社*位于某种重要的神秘通道沿线，来自一个或多个界域的超自然生物因此频繁出现在*结社*。底比斯*仲裁*辖区的迪迪马*结社*（Didyma）通过"圣道"与米勒图斯城相连。这是一条重要的凡俗道路，同时也是从*妖精*界域通往凡俗世界的主要路线，*妖精*经常在此出没（*Through the Aegis*，第 4 章）。

**Superiors:** The player characters can be given orders by the ranking members of the covenant, which they must obey.

**上级**：*结社*的高级成员可以向玩家角色下达命令，而玩家角色必须服从。

**Unknown:** An Unknown Hook is taken on by one of the players, who will act as storyguide for that aspect of the saga. The other players have no idea what sort of stories this Hook will generate. This must be something that the characters could realistically be unaware of.

The player who will storyguide the Hook chooses a Minor Hook to be the unknown Hook, but this counts as a Major Hook for the purposes of balancing Boons. Note that, if you do not want to play troupe-style, Unknown Hooks are inappropriate.

**未知**：未知羁绊由某位玩家承担，该玩家将担任*传奇*这一方面的*指引者*。其他玩家完全不知道这个羁绊会引发何种故事。这必须是角色在现实中可能确实不知情的事物。

担任该羁绊*指引者*的玩家选择一个次要羁绊作为未知羁绊，但在平衡契机时，它计作主要羁绊。请注意，若不想以*剧团式*风格游玩，未知羁绊并不适合使用。

**Urban:** The covenant is in a city. The vast majority of the inhabitants are not part of the covenant, and the covenant does not rule the city.

**城市**：*结社*位于一座城市之中。绝大多数居民并非*结社*成员，而*结社*也不统治该城市。
