## Animal Spells

## 动物法术 (Animal Spells)

Animal spells affect all natural living things that are not plants or humans, doing to animals what Mentem and Corpus spells do to people. Animal spells may affect any animal-like mind or body. They also affect things made with animal products, such as leather, bone, or wool.

动物 (Animal) 法术影响所有非植物、非人类的自然生物；它们对动物的作用，类似心灵 (Mentem) 与肉身 (Corpus) 法术对人的作用。动物法术可以影响任何类似动物的心智或身体，也能影响由动物制品制成的东西，例如皮革、骨头或羊毛。

To tell if an Animal spell will affect the mind of a creature, determine from the animal's Characteristics if it has Cunning or Intelligence. If the creature has Intelligence, a Mentem spell must be used. If it has Cunning, an Animal spell must be used. When a creature or person who has an Intelligence Characteristic is shapechanged, Animal spells that affect the mind are not effective. Instead, Mentem spells are needed. However, the body of such a being may be affected by Animal spells, including spells like *The Falcon's Hood* (PeAn 20).

要判断动物法术是否会影响生物的心智，应查看该动物的属性 (Characteristics)，确定它具有狡智 (Cunning) 还是智力 (Intelligence)。如果该生物具有智力，必须使用心灵法术；如果它具有狡智，必须使用动物法术。当具有智力属性的生物或人处于变形状态时，影响心智的动物法术无效，必须改用心灵法术。不过，这类存在的身体仍可能受到动物法术影响，包括 *鹰首罩* (The Falcon's Hood, PeAn 20) 这样的法术。

If a human body has animal parts (such as bat wings), those parts can be affected by Animal spells, even though the rest of the body is only affected by Corpus spells.

如果人类的身体拥有动物部分（如蝙蝠翅膀），这些部分可能会受到动物法术的影响，即使身体的其余部分仅受肉身法术的影响。

A Bjornaer magus in animal form is truly an animal, so his mind is affected by Animal spells rather than Mentem spells, and his body by Animal spells rather than Corpus spells. However, spells with continuing effects cast on one form continue to affect the other. Most shapechangers do not change their essential nature, which remains human, so their bodies can still be affected by Corpus spells, as well as by Animal.

处于动物形态的比约奈尔 (Bjornaer) 法师是真正的动物，因此他的心智受动物法术而非心灵法术影响，身体也受动物法术而非肉身法术影响。不过，施加在一种形态上的持续效果法术会继续影响另一种形态。大多数变形者 (shapechangers) 不会改变自身本质，他们的本质仍是人类，因此其身体仍可同时受到肉身法术和动物法术影响。

A basic Individual for Animal is an animal of about the same size as a pony, Size +1 or lower.

动物法术的基础个体 (Individual) 是一只大小与小马差不多的动物，体型 (Size) 为 +1 或更小。

### Creo Animal Guidelines

### 创生动物法术准则 (Creo Animal Guidelines)

Most Animal spells cannot be used with Personal range, so the level of a spell must be higher than the guidelines given.

大多数动物法术不能使用个人 (Personal) 距离，因此法术的等级必须高于给出的准则。

To create treated animal products (like leather, jointed meat, and cloth) add one magnitude to the level necessary to create the equivalent amount of dead animal. To create treated and processed animal products, such as a leather jacket or a woolen tunic, add two magnitudes to the level necessary to create the equivalent amount of dead animal.

要创造经过处理的动物制品（如皮革、分割肉和布料），在创造等量死体动物所需等级的基础上增加一个量级 (magnitude)。要创造经过处理和加工的动物制品，例如皮夹克或羊毛长袍，在创造等量死体动物所需等级的基础上增加两个量级。

In order to influence the behavior of created animals, a Rego requisite is required, and as this is an additional effect it adds at least one magnitude to the spell level. Otherwise they just do what comes naturally.

为了影响所创造动物的行为，需要一个掌控 (Rego) 必备技法 (requisite)，且因为这是额外效果，它会使法术等级至少增加一个量级。否则它们只会做符合天性的事情。

Creo Animal botches often produce the wrong sort of animal, or produce an animal enraged with the caster.

创生动物大失败通常会产生错误种类的动物，或产生一只对施法者暴怒的动物。

### Intellego Animal Guidelines

### 全视动物法术准则 (Intellego Animal Guidelines)

When you try to get information from an animal, remember that its memories are gathered through its particular senses, with their particular strengths and weaknesses. For example, dogs remember much about odors of various things, but often forget details of how they looked. Animals do not remember human words, but might remember tone of voice and such. You may not get the exact information you are looking for.

当你试图从动物身上获取信息时，请记住，它们的记忆是通过各自特有的感官收集的，而这些感官各有强弱。例如，狗会记住许多事物的气味，却常常忘记它们的外观细节。动物不记得人类的话语，但可能记得语气之类的信息。你未必能获得自己正在寻找的确切信息。

To communicate with an animal, you must bring yourself to the animal's mental level — a process that might have strange, though temporary, effects on you. For example, you might have a craving for raw meat or speak very slowly for a moment after communicating with a bear.

为了与动物交流，你必须将自己置于动物的心智水平——这个过程可能会对你产生奇怪（尽管是暂时）的影响。例如，在与熊交流后，你可能会想吃生肉，或者在片刻之内说话非常缓慢。

Since Animal includes aspects of both Corpus and Mentem with regard to beasts, guidelines that apply to the latter two may apply to Animal. InCo and InMe may be referred to for additional ideas.

由于就野兽而言，动物 (Animal) 同时包含肉身 (Corpus) 与心灵 (Mentem) 的面向，因此适用于后两者的准则也可能适用于动物。可以参考全视肉身 (InCo) 和全视心灵 (InMe) 获取更多灵感。

Botches with InAn normally result in false information.

全视动物大失败通常会导致错误信息。

### Muto Animal Guidelines

### 万变动物法术准则 (Muto Animal Guidelines)

Animals granted new abilities, such as wings, will need a few minutes to get used to them. When transforming things, you can transform things that they are carrying with appropriate requisites. For example, you could transform a horse and its armor with a Terram requisite.

被赋予新能力（例如翅膀）的动物需要几分钟来适应。变形目标时，你可以借助适当的必备技法一并改变它们携带的物品。例如，你可以通过地 (Terram) 必备技法来变形一匹马及其盔甲。

Animals are not infinitely mutable, and using too many of these spells for too long on an animal can warp its natural constitution and even destroy it. Muto Corpus spells may be examined to get additional ideas and guidelines for Muto Animal Spells. Common sigils seen in Muto Animal spells involve animals being changed in some strange way. Beasts transformed by Julian of Flambeau, with *The Beast Remade* (MuAn 25), might have bright red hair in their new form, while toads created by Silva of Merinita, with *Transformation of the Ravenous Beast to the Torpid Toad* (MuAn 30), might have a comical coloration.

动物并非可以无限变化；对一只动物使用过多此类法术且维持过久，可能扭曲其自然体质，甚至毁灭它。可以查阅万变肉身法术，以获得更多关于万变动物法术的思路和准则。万变动物法术中常见的法师印记，是让动物以某种奇怪方式发生变化。被弗兰博的朱利安 (Julian of Flambeau) 用 *重塑灵躯* (The Beast Remade, MuAn 25) 变形的野兽，在新形态下可能会有鲜红色毛发；而由梅里尼塔的西尔瓦 (Silva of Merinita) 用 *变蛙术* (Transformation of the Ravenous Beast to the Torpid Toad, MuAn 30) 创造的蟾蜍，可能会带有滑稽的色彩。

MuAn botches often change the caster instead of the target, or change the target in a way very different from that intended.

万变动物大失败通常会改变施法者而不是目标，或者以非常不同于预期的方式改变目标。

湮灭动物法术通常不能用吸收 (Soak) 抵御——魔法并未创造出造成伤害的东西，而是直接造成伤害。魔法抗力 (Magic Resistance) 是抵御它的唯一方法。

发明新法术时，你可能希望参考湮灭肉身 (Perdo Corpus) 获取准则和灵感。

##### Ward against the Beasts of Legend

##### 御幻兽结界 (Ward against the Beasts of Legend)

R: Touch, D: Ring, T: Circle<br>
距离：接触，持续时间：环，目标：圆环<br>

No magical beast whose Magic Might is equal to or less than the level can affect anyone within the Circle or, if it is within the Circle, affect those outside. When a magus is making this and most other wards, a common gesture employed is the Fig Sign. The magus balls his or her right hand into a fist, placing the thumb between the index and middle fingers. A magus traveling to Italy should be warned that this sign is a deadly insult in that country.<br>
任何魔法威能小于或等于该等级的魔法野兽，都无法影响结界内的任何人；如果它位于结界内，也无法影响结界外的人。法师施展此类结界以及大多数其他结界时，常用的手势是“无花果手势”：法师将右手握成拳，将拇指夹在食指与中指之间。前往意大利旅行的法师应当被提醒，这种手势在当地是极其严重的侮辱。<br>

##### Mastering the Unruly Beast

##### 慑服狂兽 (Mastering the Unruly Beast)

R: Touch, D: Conc, T: Ind<br>
距离：接触，持续时间：专注，目标：个体<br>

You can make an animal perform any act it is capable of. You can only issue new commands while touching the animal, but the animal will carry them out without requiring constant contact. Further, the animal will allow you to touch it throughout the duration of the spell. It is difficult to maintain control of particularly stubborn or fierce animals, such as mules or boars — creatures with appropriate Personality Traits may roll against an Ease Factor of 12 each round. When Chavin of Tytalus casts this spell, the creature acts surly and unfriendly while going about its tasks, even when Chavin doesn't specifically tell it to act that way.<br>
你能让动物做出它力所能及的任何行为。你只能在触摸动物时发出新命令，但动物执行命令时不需要持续接触。此外，在法术持续期间，动物会允许你触摸它。要维持对特别顽固或凶猛动物（如骡子或野猪）的控制很困难——具有适当性格特征 (Personality Traits) 的生物每轮都可以对抗难度等级 12 进行掷骰。当泰塔罗斯的查文施展此法术时，该生物执行任务时会表现得乖戾而不友好，即使查文并未特别命令它如此。<br>

(Base 15, +1 Touch, +1 Concentration)
（基础等级 15，+1 接触，+1 专注）

你向动物植入一道复杂命令，它会尽己所能去执行。该命令必须涉及完成某项特定任务，例如找到某个特定的人。像“保护我”这样模糊的命令不起作用。如果动物在法术即将结束前仍未完成任务，它会变得极其凶猛，尤其是在夜间。<br>

(Base 15, +1 Touch, +2 Sun)
