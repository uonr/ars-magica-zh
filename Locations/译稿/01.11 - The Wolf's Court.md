### The Wolf's Court
### 群狼法庭 (The Wolf's Court)

**Geographic Location**: In the depths of a dense forest
**地理位置 (Geographic Location)**：茂密森林的深处

**Tribunal**: Any, nominally Provençal Tribunal.
**仲裁 (Tribunal)**：任何仲裁，名义上属于普罗旺斯仲裁 (Provençal Tribunal)。

**Overview**: There is a cairn in a remote spot in a wolf-haunted forest. It is avoided by all locals, who consider the place cursed. However, sometimes when a man has been wronged, and in his search for justice has been failed by the manor court, the bishop's court, and appeals to the saints, he will come to this cairn at the rising of the full moon and ask the wolves for the justice he has been denied elsewhere.
**概述 (Overview)**：在狼群出没的森林中，有一处偏僻的石冢。所有当地人都避开它，认为这个地方被诅咒了。然而，有时当一个人蒙受冤屈，在寻求正义的过程中又被庄园法庭、主教法庭以及向圣徒的祈求所辜负时，他会在满月升起时来到这个石冢，向狼群请求他在别处被剥夺的正义。

Characters might wonder at the wolves' capacity to enforce justice. Those who investigate might uncover secrets reaching back as far as Ancient Rome, and attract the unwanted attention of those dedicated to preserving those secrets.
角色们可能会对狼群执行正义的能力感到好奇。那些进行调查的人可能会发现远至古罗马时期的秘密，并引起那些致力于保守这些秘密的人不必要的注意。
