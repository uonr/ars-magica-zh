### The Poisoned Battlefield
### 剧毒古战场 (The Poisoned Battlefield)

**Geographic Location**: The Mendip Hills, or wherever Roman miners were once found.
**地理位置 (Geographic Location)**：门迪普丘陵（Mendip Hills），或任何曾经发现过罗马矿工的地方。

**Tribunal**: Stonehenge Tribunal.
**仲裁 (Tribunal)**：巨石阵仲裁 (Stonehenge Tribunal)。

**Overview**: This site is no longer blighted, some magi say. The archnecromancer no longer lives here. The ghosts of dead druids no longer walk here. The miners all died screaming centuries ago, so they are no threat. The poisoned soil is covered with a thin layer of verdant green.
**概述 (Overview)**：一些法师说，这个地方已不再被诅咒枯萎。大死灵法师不再住在这里了。死去的德鲁伊幽灵也不再在这里游荡。矿工们几个世纪前就在尖叫中死去了，所以他们构不成威胁。被毒化的土壤上覆盖着薄薄一层翠绿。

A very thin layer of green.
非常薄的一层绿色。
