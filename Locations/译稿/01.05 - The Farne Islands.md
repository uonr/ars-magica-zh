### The Farne Islands
### 法恩群岛 (The Farne Islands)

**Geographic Location**: A small archipelago of islands.
**地理位置 (Geographic Location)**：一个小群岛。

**Tribunal**: Stonehenge Tribunal.
**仲裁 (Tribunal)**：巨石阵仲裁 (Stonehenge Tribunal)。

**Overview**: Inner Farne was famously the home to a saint; according to his legend he drove out the demons who tormented the locals from Inner Farne to some of the islands lying further out to sea. Today, the influence of the saint is still very much present on the inner islands, whereas the outer islands are home to wilder forces.
**概述 (Overview)**：内法恩岛（Inner Farne）以作为一位圣徒的家园而闻名；根据他的传说，他将折磨当地人的恶魔从内法恩岛驱赶到了离海更远的一些岛屿上。如今，这位圣徒的影响力在内层岛屿上仍然非常明显，而外层岛屿则是更狂分野性力量的家园。

There are several mysteries to solve amongst the islands, from the politics of the selkie courts to the identity of the mysterious inhabitant of Crumstone Rock. With stories drawn from all four supernatural Realms, these islands are sure to be of interest to members of the Order of Hermes.
在群岛中仍有几个谜团需要解开，从海豹精（selkie）宫廷的政治到克拉姆斯通岩（Crumstone Rock）神秘居民的身份。这些岛屿汇集了来自所有四个超自然界域（Realms）的故事，赫尔墨斯秘会（Order of Hermes）的成员定会对其产生兴趣。
