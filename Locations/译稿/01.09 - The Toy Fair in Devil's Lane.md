### The Toy Fair in Devil's Lane
### 魔鬼巷玩具集市 (The Toy Fair in Devil's Lane)

**Geographic Location:** In a faerie regio. There are entrances in many cities.
**地理位置 (Geographic Location)**：位于一个妖精灵阶（faerie regio）中。在许多城市都有入口。

**Tribunal:** Any.
**仲裁 (Tribunal)**：任何仲裁。

**Overview:** The toy fair is a faerie regio that has entrances in many cities, selling curious magical and miraculous toys to the cityfolk. A Merinita covenant is built within the fair, but the toys are actually made by elves hidden in workshops in the basements of the shops. The player characters might stumble upon the fair while exploring a city, or find (and trace) magic items from the workshop in the hands of mundane characters. The covenant is not a secret and the Redcaps know how to get in.
**概述 (Overview)**：玩具集市是一个在许多城市都有入口的妖精灵阶，向市民出售奇特魔法和奇迹般的玩具。集市内部建有一个梅里尼塔学派（Merinita）结社，但玩具实际上是由隐藏在商店地下室工坊里的精灵（elves）制作的。玩家角色可能会在探索城市时偶然发现集市，或者在凡俗角色的手中发现（并追踪）来自工坊的魔法物品。这个结社并不是什么秘密，红披风（Redcaps）知道如何进去。

The player characters might visit the toy fair to buy or commission items, or to take advantage of the regio to travel to a distant Tribunal. The multiple entrances in many Tribunals also create questions of Hermetic jurisdiction over the covenant. The player characters could also decide to join, or usurp, the toy fair covenant.
玩家角色可能会参观玩具集市以购买或定制物品，或者利用灵阶前往遥远的仲裁。在许多仲裁中的多个入口也引发了关于对该结社赫尔墨斯管辖权的问题。玩家角色也可以决定加入，或者篡夺玩具集市结社。
