### The Truffle Matron
### 松露主母 (The Truffle Matron)

**Geographic Location**: A small forest near Dijon in Burgundy, the dense forests of Périgord in Aquitaine, the Istrian peninsula in Croatia, or the Bohemian Forest.
**地理位置 (Geographic Location)**：勃艮第第戎附近的一小片森林、阿基坦佩里戈尔的茂密森林、克罗地亚的伊斯特拉半岛，或波希米亚森林。

**Tribunal**: Normandy, Transylvania, or Rhine Tribunals.
**仲裁 (Tribunal)**：诺曼底（Normandy）、特兰西瓦尼亚（Transylvania）或莱茵仲裁（Rhine Tribunals）。

**Overview:** Mythic Europeans do not know why the delicious, aromatic truffle only grows under certain trees. They only know that truffles propagate through some supernatural method and that harvesting them may result in interaction with supernatural powers. Truffle hunters use animals to locate their prize because supernatural forces might punish them for improperly harvesting a truffle. Truffle hunters may attempt to appease the truffle guardians, but if their efforts are unsuccessful, they hope that the animal will suffer the punishment. Hunters face these dangers because the truffles they collect are often worth their weight in gold.
**概述 (Overview)**：神话欧洲人不知道为什么美味芳香的松露只生长在某些树下。他们只知道松露通过某种超自然方法繁殖，采收它们可能会导致与超自然力量的互动。松露猎人利用动物来寻找他们的战利品，因为超自然力量可能会惩罚他们不当采收松露。松露猎人可能会试图安抚松露守卫，但如果他们的努力没有成功，他们希望动物会遭受惩罚。猎人们之所以面临这些危险，是因为他们收集的松露往往价值连城。

This chapter discusses one of the sources of truffles in Mythic Europe. Characters visiting the Matron of the Truffles are presented with the opportunity to enhance their knowledge and magical power through interaction with her subjects.
本章讨论了神话欧洲中松露的来源之一。拜访松露主母（Matron of the Truffles）的角色们可以通过与她的臣民互动，获得增进知识和魔法力量的机会。
